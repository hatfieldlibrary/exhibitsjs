Vernor began school at Willamette University as a sophomore, joining his younger brother Sheldon in the fall of 1919. The young men were very involved at the small university, joining organizations such as the Websterian Society, Sigma Tau fraternity, and the student newspaper the Collegian. The brothers both studied education. They graduated in 1922. Most of Vernor's photographs can be found in the Wallulahs, the student yearbooks.
